About Us!


Chef4U, founded and passionately led by Dhwani Shah in Ahmedabad, has been delighting guests with exceptional catering for over 10 years. What started as a small dream has grown into one of the city's most trusted full-service catering brands. We specialize in all types of events, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, with a minimum order for 100+ guests.

At Chef4U, we believe every event deserves unforgettable food and service. Our experienced team curates customized menus that blend traditional flavors with modern creativity, using fresh, high-quality ingredients. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, family celebration, or festive occasion, we bring the perfect touch of taste, presentation, and hospitality to every table.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, timely service, and the joy our food brings to guests. With a decade of experience and thousands of satisfied customers, Chef4U is here to make your special moments even more delicious.
